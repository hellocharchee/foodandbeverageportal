Welcome to The Brass Phoenix

The Brass Phoenix Pub is a loving tribute to the classic neighborhood tavern. For years, we've been the pulsating heart of the local craft beer scene—a warm, welcoming sanctuary where great food, stiff drinks, and clinking glasses echo into the early hours.

We've elevated that classic pub magic. From the exposed brick walls and glowing patio string lights to our meticulously curated rotating taps, The Brass Phoenix is designed to be your home away from home.

To preserve this immersive atmosphere, we realized our menu needed to be as seamless as our hospitality. We integrated this frictionless digital hub so our patrons spend less time deciphering complex, cluttered menus, and more time where it matters—connecting with friends, family, and the rhythm of the night.
